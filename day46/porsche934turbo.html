Porsche 934 Turbo

The Porsche 934 Turbo is a racing version of the Porsche 911 Turbo, designed for FIA Group 4 rules. Introduced in 1976, it was manufactured for two years, with a total of 31 units produced. The 934 Turbo is a unique blend of production car and racing machine, featuring a stock 930 bodyshell with added reinforcements and a turbocharged engine producing over 500 horsepower.
