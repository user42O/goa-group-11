About Porsche

Founded in 1931, Porsche is renowned for its precision engineering and iconic designs, epitomizing automotive excellence with models like the Porsche 911.

Models

Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a timeless sports car known for its rear-engine layout and iconic design.

Porsche Cayenne

The Porsche Cayenne is a luxury SUV that combines performance with practicality.

Porsche Panamera

The Porsche Panamera is a four-door luxury sedan offering both comfort and speed.

History

Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the company initially offered vehicle development work and consulting, but quickly expanded into producing its own vehicles.
